# Major Denial Categories (Appeal Strategy Overview)

## Authorization
- **Typical codes**: CO197, N290, N361 (authorization required, missing prior auth, auth expired).
- **Strategy**: Request retro authorization; submit referral, order, and supporting documentation. Include ordering provider details and cite payer authorization policy when prior auth was not required or was obtained.

## Medical necessity
- **Typical codes**: CO50, CO97, CO167, M51, N362, N382.
- **Strategy**: Submit clinical documentation (progress notes, treatment plan, labs) that supports necessity. Reference payer medical policy, LCD, or NCD and show the patient meets criteria.

## Documentation
- **Typical codes**: CO16, CO252, N217, N306, N417 (missing info, attachment missing, insufficient documentation).
- **Strategy**: Submit corrected or supplemental documentation (e.g., progress notes, operative report) and resubmit or appeal with the complete record.

## Coding / billing
- **Typical codes**: CO11, CO55, CO125, CO181, CO246, N95, N211, N370 (diagnosis/procedure mismatch, bundling, billing error, invalid CPT, modifier).
- **Strategy**: Correct the claim and resubmit when the error is clear; otherwise appeal with the medical record and explanation of how documentation supports the codes and modifiers.

## Timely filing
- **Typical codes**: CO29, N123.
- **Strategy**: Provide proof of timely submission (clearinghouse report, confirmation, date of first submission). If the denial was in error, appeal with that proof.

## Eligibility / coverage
- **Typical codes**: CO109, CO26, CO27, N432, N522, N523 (coverage terminated, expenses before/after coverage).
- **Strategy**: Verify eligibility and correct payer/date; submit eligibility verification and correct claim or appeal with evidence of coverage.

## Duplicate claim
- **Typical codes**: CO18, CO222, M86, N86.
- **Strategy**: Provide proof the claim is not a duplicate (original claim number, EOB, documentation that service was performed once).

## Benefit limit
- **Typical codes**: CO119, N362 (benefit maximum, policy limitation).
- **Strategy**: Request reconsideration with medical justification; attach clinical notes and any policy or exception documentation.
